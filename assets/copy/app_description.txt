### Streamlined password management, simplicity and top-notch security

**Streamlined UI.** Native to iOS, it provides an elegant and user-friendly experience. **Local Security.** Your digital keys stay on your device, shielding them from online threats. **Private Network Sync.** Safely access passwords on your devices without the internet. **Organized Access.** Easily manage passwords, Authenticator codes, WiFi details, private notes, and credit card info. **Authenticator Widget.** Quickly access codes from your home screen for faster authentication. **Biometric Quick Entry.** Use Face ID or Touch ID for secure and immediate access. **Offline Peace of Mind.** No cloud involvement reduces digital exposure, ensuring complete security for your sensitive data.

**Designed exclusively for iOS, Pocket Pass Manager brings straightforward security to your fingertips.**
